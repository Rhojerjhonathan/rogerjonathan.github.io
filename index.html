FAQs

Questions, answered.

How do I purchase flight tickets from Aero Gate?+
Submit a flight enquiry through our website, phone, WhatsApp, or email. Once we share available options and pricing, we guide you through every step of the booking and issuance process.
How do I make a payment?+
Payments are made securely through our Payhere gateway on the website, or via bank transfer as advised by our team. Your ticket is issued only after full payment confirmation.
When will I receive my e-ticket?+
After payment confirmation, e-tickets are issued and delivered to your inbox promptly — typically within a short time, subject to airline confirmation and availability.
Can I choose my preferred airline and timing?+
Yes. We present options matching your preferred airline, travel dates, and timings — subject to availability — and you make the final choice.
Do you offer airport transfers?+
Yes. Airport pickups and drop-offs are available as an add-on service. Request this at the time of booking or enquiry and we'll arrange it seamlessly.
